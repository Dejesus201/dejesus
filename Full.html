Favorite Places


Hello! This are my Favorite places that I have been and I want to go to if there is a chance. since I was a kid I always dream about being wealthy and go to places not only in this country but around the world. and now that Im near, I kept holding that dream because of how close I am from grabing it.

Palawan

Palawan is one of the places that I dreamed to go to. My Mom have been there and she said it has a good and relaxing ambiance near the ocean. Seeing it in the photo that my Mom took, I was amazed and fall inlove in its clear seawater and white sand. she also mention that there are only few tourist that hang in there so the area is quiet perfect for me to relax.





Kalibo, Aklan

Since I came back here in the urban city and start studying from my senior high up until now, I always think about going back to Kalibo, Aklan. I sometimes lived there because my Father was raised there. And Everytime we visit my Grandparents we always timed in the Ati-Atihan festival which is the most fun festival that you could ever attend. I just missed the feeling of it so I think of this as my favorite places to go back.





Japan

This time its not around the Philippines. This one is in my ultimate bucket list. Japan is the place that I want to go and spend months this place sometimes inspires me to move forward towards my goals. this place has its amazing places to wonder. Its the place that I could settle for a long time.
